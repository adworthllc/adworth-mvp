02 Description of Services

  • adworth.app — Public-facing homepage, waitlist signup, and company information

04 Third-Party Data & AI Analysis

Our Ad Intelligence Dashboard processes data from external sources using third-party APIs. By using the dashboard, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

SerpApi — Google Ads Transparency Center data

Ad data displayed in our dashboard is retrieved in real time from Google's Ads Transparency Center via SerpApi (serpapi.com). This data includes advertiser names, ad creative identifiers, ad formats, campaign dates, and targeting domain information as made publicly available by Google.

  • We do not store raw SerpApi results permanently. Results may be cached temporarily in Cloudflare Workers KV (AD_CACHE namespace) for performance, with a cache TTL of 1–2 hours.
  • SerpApi's Terms of Service apply to the underlying data retrieval. SerpApi's terms are available at serpapi.com/legal.
  • Google's Ads Transparency Center data is publicly available information. Our use of it is for transparency and research purposes.

Anthropic API — AI privacy analysis

Privacy risk scoring, transparency scoring, consent gap identification, regulatory flag detection, and AI-generated findings displayed in our dashboard are generated by Anthropic's Claude AI (anthropic.com) via the Anthropic API.

  • Ad data retrieved from SerpApi is sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. This data consists of publicly available advertiser and ad metadata — no personal user data is transmitted.
  • AI-generated analysis is probabilistic. Scores, findings, and regulatory flags are illustrative assessments, not legal determinations.
  • Anthropic's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy apply to AI processing. These are available at anthropic.com/legal.

Cloudflare Workers — Infrastructure

All server-side processing — including ad data retrieval, AI analysis, consent token issuance and validation, and waitlist form handling — runs on Cloudflare Workers edge infrastructure. Cloudflare's Terms of Service apply to this processing and are available at cloudflare.com/terms.

No liability for third-party data

Adworth℠ LLC is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of data provided by SerpApi, Google, Anthropic, or any other third-party source. We make no representations about the correctness of analysis results.

06 Consent Token System

Our cryptographic consent token API is currently in beta/demonstration phase. The following terms apply to its use:

Current state

The consent validation API at adworth-dsa-api.adworthllc.workers.dev and the ad intelligence pipeline at adworth-ads.adworthllc.workers.dev currently demonstrate token issuance, validation, and real-time ad transparency analysis. They are not yet connected to a live advertiser marketplace or payment system. All token transactions at this stage are for demonstration purposes only.

No financial transactions

The current consent token system does not process real financial transactions. Any references to user compensation or advertiser payments on adworth.app describe the intended future functionality of the Adworth consent marketplace, which has not yet launched.
