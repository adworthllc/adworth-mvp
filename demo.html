How to Test
1
Install the extension

Load the Privacy Cockpit extension from chrome://extensions (developer mode).

2
Click Setup Extension

Generates an Ed25519 keypair on your device. Your private key never leaves your browser.

3
Click Sync Dashboard Data

The extension extracts the simulated metrics above (standing in for your real advertising dashboard), signs them with your device's private key, and sends the signed payload to the Adworth Ingestion API for cryptographic verification and storage.

4
Verify in the admin dashboard

Visit adworth-ingestion-api.adworthllc.workers.dev/dashboard to see the ingested, signature-verified data.
