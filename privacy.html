The short version: Adworth is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. We collect only what we need to operate the service, we never sell your data, and you can delete it at any time. Our Ad Intelligence Dashboard retrieves public ad data via SerpApi and analyzes it using Anthropic's AI — no personal user data is involved in that pipeline. We do not use third-party trackers, advertising pixels, or analytics services. That's kind of the whole point.

01 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Any other information you choose to share with us

Information collected automatically

When you visit adworth.app, our infrastructure (Cloudflare and Vercel) may automatically log standard request metadata including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and timestamps
  • Referring URL
  • IP address (used for routing and security; not retained by us)
  • General geographic location (country/region level only, derived from IP)

We do not use any third-party analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics tools on adworth.app. No external analytics code runs on our pages.

Information processed through the Ad Intelligence Dashboard

When you use our Ad Intelligence Dashboard (adworth.app/dashboard.html), the following data is processed:

  • The domain name you enter for analysis (e.g. apple.com)
  • Publicly available ad data retrieved from Google's Ads Transparency Center via SerpApi — this includes advertiser names, ad creative IDs, formats, campaign dates, and targeting domains
  • AI-generated analysis of that public ad data, produced by Anthropic's Claude API

No personal user data is transmitted to SerpApi or Anthropic as part of dashboard analysis. The pipeline processes publicly available advertiser data only. Results may be cached temporarily in Cloudflare Workers KV (AD_CACHE namespace) for up to 2 hours for performance.

Information collected through our consent API

When you interact with our consent token system, we collect and process:

  • Consent token identifiers (cryptographic hashes — not linked to your identity)
  • Consent scope parameters (advertiser, data type, time window) as specified by you
  • Timestamps of consent issuance, revocation, and expiry
  • Ledger entries recording consent lifecycle events

Consent tokens are designed so that no personally identifiable information is embedded in them. Token identifiers are cryptographic values only.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial account numbers, or health data through our public-facing properties.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Adworth platform and services
  • Send you waitlist updates and product announcements (unsubscribe at any time)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Process and validate cryptographic consent tokens as requested by you
  • Maintain the tamper-evident consent ledger on your behalf
  • Power the Ad Intelligence Dashboard with real-time ad transparency data and AI analysis
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for behavioral advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles from your activity on our platform.

03 Third-Party Data Processing

Our Ad Intelligence Dashboard uses two third-party APIs to deliver real-time ad transparency analysis. This section discloses exactly how those integrations work.

SerpApi — ad data retrieval

Ad data displayed in the dashboard is retrieved in real time from Google's Ads Transparency Center via SerpApi (serpapi.com). When you enter a domain for analysis, we send that domain name to SerpApi's API, which returns publicly available ad data from Google's transparency database.

  • Data transmitted to SerpApi: the domain name you entered (e.g. nike.com)
  • Data returned: advertiser names, ad creative IDs, formats, campaign date ranges, targeting domains — all publicly available information
  • No personal user data is transmitted to SerpApi
  • Results are cached temporarily in Cloudflare Workers KV for up to 2 hours
  • SerpApi's privacy policy: serpapi.com/privacy

Anthropic API — AI privacy analysis

Privacy risk scores, transparency scores, consent gap analysis, regulatory flags, and AI findings displayed in the dashboard are generated by Anthropic's Claude AI via the Anthropic API (anthropic.com).

  • Data transmitted to Anthropic: the publicly available ad metadata retrieved from SerpApi (advertiser names, ad counts, formats, dates)
  • No personal user data, no waitlist emails, and no consent token data is transmitted to Anthropic
  • AI analysis results are not stored permanently — they are generated on demand and may be temporarily cached
  • Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy

Cloudflare Workers — server infrastructure

All server-side logic — including the ad intelligence pipeline, consent token API, and waitlist form processing — runs on Cloudflare Workers edge infrastructure. Cloudflare processes request metadata as part of serving and securing our services.

  • Waitlist email addresses are stored in Cloudflare Workers KV
  • Ad data cache is stored in Cloudflare Workers KV (AD_CACHE namespace, TTL 1–2 hours)
  • Consent ledger entries are stored in Cloudflare Workers KV
  • Cloudflare's privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

04 How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

Infrastructure providers

As described in Section 03, we use Cloudflare Workers, SerpApi, and Anthropic as infrastructure providers to deliver our services. These providers process data only as necessary to provide the service and are not permitted to use it for their own advertising or marketing purposes.

We also use:

  • Vercel (vercel.com) — Our primary website files for adworth.app are hosted and deployed via Vercel. Vercel may retain standard server access logs. Vercel's privacy policy: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Cloudflare DNS & Security — We use Cloudflare for DNS management, DDoS protection, and traffic routing.
  • Google Fonts — Our pages load typography from Google Fonts, resulting in a network request to Google's servers. We are evaluating self-hosting fonts to eliminate this dependency.
  • IONOS — Used for system email only (no-reply@adworth.app). Not used for website hosting.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Adworth, our users, or the public.

Business transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

05 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

  • Waitlist email addresses are stored in Cloudflare Workers KV until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Ad intelligence cache (SerpApi + Anthropic results) is stored temporarily in Cloudflare Workers KV with a TTL of 1–2 hours and then automatically purged.
  • Consent ledger entries are retained as part of the tamper-evident audit trail. These entries contain cryptographic identifiers only — not personal information.
  • Consent tokens are invalidated upon revocation or expiry.

When you request deletion of your personal data, we will remove it from our active systems within 30 days and from backup systems within 90 days.

06 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@adworth.app. We will respond within 30 days.

07 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS enforced via Cloudflare), cryptographic signing of consent tokens, append-only ledger design to prevent tampering, and access controls on all stored data.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at hello@adworth.app.

08 Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary to operate our website. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, behavioral analytics scripts, or any other third-party tracking technologies.

Cloudflare may set a cookie (__cf_bm) for bot management and security purposes. This cookie does not track you across sites and is not used for advertising.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

09 Children's Privacy

Adworth's services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
