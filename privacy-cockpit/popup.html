Before you set up

Setup generates a private cryptographic key on this device. That key is your only proof of identity — it is what lets you issue, view, and revoke your consent. Adworth never sees it and stores no password, email, or name for you.

If you lose this device or its key, it cannot be recovered or reset by anyone, including Adworth. You would lose access to your consent records from this device. Setting up on more than one device is the recommended safeguard.
