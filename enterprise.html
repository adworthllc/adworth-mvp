Patent-Pending Technology

Five components.
One infrastructure layer.

Filed February 23, 2026. 11 independent claims. The system makes consent mathematically binding — not a promise, a proof.

🔑

Cryptographic Consent Token

Users sign permission slips with cryptographic keys. Bound to a specific advertiser, data type, and time window. Cannot be faked, extended, or transferred.

🛡

Privacy Governance Engine

Every data access request is intercepted and verified automatically. No valid token? Blocked. Expired window? Blocked. Zero manual review required.

📖

Invisibility Ledger

Append-only, tamper-evident record of every consent, revocation, and deletion. Companies cannot claim ignorance. Cryptographic proof at every step.

🔎

State-Persistence Monitor

After deletion, the monitor keeps checking. It crawls data brokers and third-party systems to detect unauthorized data persistence post-revocation.

⚡

Removal Payload Engine

Detects violations and auto-generates legally-cited deletion notices — signed with cryptographic proof — citing GDPR, CCPA, and DSA by article number.

💰

Consent Marketplace

Advertisers pay for verified access. Users receive direct payment when tokens are validated. Revoke consent, payments stop. Deletion is enforced. Fair exchange.

Founder

Why I Left After 18 Years

I left Verizon in December 2025 after 18 years — the last 15 as a supervisor managing large technical support teams. For 15 of those years I listened to escalated calls from customers who had been financially hurt, legally exposed, emotionally devastated — almost always because of what happened to their data.

My understanding of copyright law and music publishing taught me this pattern early. Ownership extraction follows the same playbook across industries. Taylor Swift had to re-record her entire catalog just to own what she created. You don’t own your data either. Adworth changes that.

“What if users could lend their data — on their terms, with cryptographic enforcement, and get paid for it?”

I built it with a high school diploma, 20 years of corporate experience, and AI as my co-founder. The patent was filed February 23, 2026. Now I’m raising $1.5M to build it properly.
